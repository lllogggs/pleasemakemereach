Privacy Policy

Tripdotdot (hereinafter referred to as the 'Service') values the user's personal information and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act. This Privacy Policy informs you of what information the Service collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Article 1 (Items of Personal Information Collected and Collection Methods)

This Service allows free access to all content without a separate membership registration process. During the use of the service, the following information may be automatically generated and collected, but it is anonymized information that cannot be used to identify an individual.

  • Cookies, access logs, access IP information, device information
  • User-inputted Trip.com URLs

Article 2 (Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information)

The Service utilizes the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement and Statistical Analysis: To understand access frequency or for statistics on members' service usage. (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • User Experience Analysis: To analyze user behavior (clicks, scrolls, etc.) within the service to improve UI/UX. (e.g., Microsoft Clarity)
  • Error Resolution and Feature Enhancement: User-inputted URLs are collected anonymously to resolve link conversion errors and to statistically analyze what types of links are primarily used, thereby improving the service.

Article 3 (Period of Retention and Use of Personal Information)

The collected anonymous information is stored for a certain period in accordance with reasons for information retention under relevant laws and is then destroyed.

Article 4 (Changes to the Privacy Policy)

Any additions, deletions, or corrections to this Privacy Policy due to changes in laws or policies will be notified through the website's announcements.
